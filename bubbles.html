About

Description

Bubbles - Time Sheet is a simple and fun alternative to the classic working time recording. Recording working hours is usually an unpopular and boring, but mostly necessary, task. Bubbles focuses on a simple, but very interactive and fun user experience to make the process of recording working time a little bit more bearable.

To start the time tracking process, simply drag a Project Bubble and then the corresponding Task Bubble to the bottom of the screen. Bubbles will do the rest! To stop the recording, simply drag the corresponding bubble back up.

The recorded times can be displayed via the calendar view. Projects and associated tasks can be easily managed.

To make the recording of times even more comfortable, Bubbles also offers a Watch App, which allows you to control the time recording process easily on your wrist.

Tech Details

Swift, WatchKit, SwiftUI, CoreData, WKExtension, Combine, Darkmode, Custom UI Components, Custom Animations, Complex Userinterface
